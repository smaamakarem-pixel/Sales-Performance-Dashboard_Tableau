# Sales Performance Dashboard (Tableau)

A sales analytics dashboard built in **Tableau**, analyzing orders, customers, products, and regional performance to track revenue and profitability KPIs.

## 📊 Overview

The dashboard connects multiple data sources (Orders, Products, Customers, Location) to deliver a unified view of sales performance across categories, regions, and time periods.

## 🔍 Key Features

- **KPI Tracking**: Dedicated KPIs for Sales, Profit, and Quantity to monitor overall business performance.
- **Year-over-Year Comparison**: Current Year (CY) Profit tracking with % difference calculations to highlight growth or decline trends.
- **Category & Sub-Category Analysis**: Breakdown of sales and profit performance across product categories and sub-categories.
- **Regional & Segment Insights**: Performance comparison across regions, states, and customer segments.
- **Interactive Filtering**: Year selection parameter allowing dynamic, time-based analysis.

## 🛠️ Tools & Skills Used

- **Tableau**: Calculated fields, KPI parameters, multi-table data relationships, dashboard design
- **Data Sources**: Orders, Products, Customers, and Location datasets (multi-table sales data model)
